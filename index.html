About me

I'm a neuroscience student interested in research into our brain on multiple levels: from neural circuits to cognitive systems.

My interest up until now has been in the brain as interpreter of sensory information, with a focus on multisensory integration.

Therefore, I wrote my Bachelor's thesis on evidence accumulation in audiovisual decisions in humans. You can read it here.

I'm aspiring to receive a Master's degree in systems neuroscience to be able to continue my career by obtaining my PhD.
