About me

I'm a neuroscience student interested in research into our brain on multiple levels: from neural systems to cognition and behaviour.

Up until now my research has mainly concerned the brain as interpreter of sensory information, with a specific focus on multisensory integration.

Therefore, I wrote my Bachelor's thesis on evidence accumulation in audiovisual decisions in humans. You can find it along with my other work.

I'm currently at the LMU workings toward receiving a Master's degree in neuroscience to be able to continue my career by obtaining my PhD.
