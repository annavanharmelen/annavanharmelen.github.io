My work

My work has always focussed around sensory processing, but has ranged from the level of neural circuits to cognitive systems.

In the second year of my Bachelor's I wrote a short review on the connections between entorhinal grid cells and surrounding neuronal populations.

A year later I studied and wrote my Bachelor's thesis on multisensory evidence accumulation in human audiovisual decisions.
